Projects

Engineering and research projects spanning CubeSat instrumentation, data pipeline development, and ionospheric science.

CU Boulder · Ongoing

COSMO — Data Processing Lead

Leading data processing for COSMO, a 6U CubeSat mission that measures Earth's magnetic field using vectorized rubidium scalars. Covers in-orbit magnetometer calibration, science data pipeline, and distribution to mission sponsors.

CubeSat Magnetometer Data Pipeline In-Orbit Calibration
NCU · Taiwan Space Agency Internship · 2023

FORMOSAT-7/COSMIC-2 GPS Receiver Anomaly Study

Investigated aberrant records from commercial off-the-shelf bus GPS receivers onboard the FORMOSAT-7/COSMIC-2 constellation, including analysis of single event effects from space weather.

GNSS Space Weather LEO Satellites FORMOSAT-7
NCU · 2021–2023

Ionospheric Response to Solar Eclipses

Analyzed in-situ satellite measurements from Swarm, FORMOSAT-7/COSMIC-2, and ICON to characterize ionospheric responses to the 21 June 2020 annular solar eclipse and the 8 April 2024 total solar eclipse.

Ionosphere Satellite Data Solar Eclipse
NCU · 2022–2023

Tonga Volcanic Eruption — Ionospheric & Magnetic Signatures

Studied ionospheric uplift and magnetic field signatures caused by tropospheric and thermospheric Lamb waves generated by the 15 January 2022 Tonga volcanic eruption.

Ionosphere Lamb Waves Geomagnetic Volcanic Eruption
