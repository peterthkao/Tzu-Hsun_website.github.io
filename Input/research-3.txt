-> Wave-Driven Particle Precipitation
--- There are many plasma waves propagating in the magnetosphere along the magnetic field, including hiss, whistler, electromagnetic ion cyclotron (EMIC), etc. Those plasma waves are oscillating variations of electric and magnetic fields within space plasma. These waves can further accelerating or scattering particles into the atmosphere and create aurora and other effects. In the space weather point of view, the energetic particle precipitation (EPP) can reach about 10s km altitude, which could affect the flight crew's health. The EPP can also cause HF communication block out during strong storm events, increasing the risk of polar flights.
