Hello! I'm Tzu-Hsun, a PhD student at the University of Colorado Boulder, Department of Aerospace Engineering Sciences. I'm a lead of COSMO data processing, a 6U CubeSat measuring Earth's magnetic field using vectorized Rubidium scalars. COSMO has launched at the end of March 2026. Now we are working on getting first science data from it. My research interest is on the magnetic field, magnetosphere, wave-driven particle precipitation, and ionospheric responses to solar and geophysical events. It's my pleasure to see you here! Please feel free to browsing around and let me know if you have any questions!
