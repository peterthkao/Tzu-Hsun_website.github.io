-> Space Instrumentation
--- To understand the space environment, we need to have measurement data to verify our theory. The space instruments can be divided into a few categories: particle detectors (ionized and neutral), magnetometers, optical instruments (UV, IR, visible light, etc.), mass spectrometers, plasma instruments, etc. Each of them can have various designs and for different purpose. Since this is an instrument in space, there are a series of tests need to be conducted before launch. The vaccum and radiation environment is also a challenge for instruments to perform normally.
