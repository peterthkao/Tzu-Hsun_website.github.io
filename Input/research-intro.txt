My research focuses on the ionosphere, magnetic field dynamics, wave-driven particle precipitation, and space instrumentation. I study how geophysical events, e.g. solar eclipses, volcanic eruptions, earthquakes, and geomagnetic storms, responses in the ionosphere. I also investigate the ElectroMagnetic Ion Cyclotron (EMIC) waves and the associated particle precipitation by using the spaceborne magnetometers and particle detectors. The other research interest is the space instrument single event effects (SEEs), which is affected by energetic particles.
