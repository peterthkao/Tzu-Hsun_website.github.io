-> Geomagnetic Field
--- The Earth's magnetic field is a strong shield that protects us from the strong solar wind and other solar events. Monitoring the magnetic field helps us understand its structure and dynamics. However, it dynamics can result in the geomagnetic storm and further ionospheric storm. The geomagnetic storm can disrupt navigation system and cause geomagnetically induced currents (GICs) in the ground power grid network. Since the magnetic field can affect our live so much, scientists created a series of indices to indicate the disturbance of the magnetic field, ex: Dst, Kp, Ap, etc. These indices are also important to the space weather application.
